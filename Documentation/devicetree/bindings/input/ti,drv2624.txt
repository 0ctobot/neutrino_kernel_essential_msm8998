* Texas Instruments - drv2624 Haptics driver

Required properties:
	- compatible - "ti,drv2624" - DRV2624
	- reg -  I2C slave address
	- ti,reset-gpio - GPIO to reset the device

Example:

drv2624: drv2624@5a {
	compatible = "ti,drv2624";
	reg = <0x5a>;
	ti,reset-gpio = <&tlmm 95 0>;
};
